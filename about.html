“United we raise mountains”

We are a duet. We believe that with a little help and the right support, nothing is impossible. The joy that comes from exercising with others, the laughs and sweat we share are uncomparable to anything else. That’s what keeps us going, that is the reason of our work as fitness trainers- we desire to unite people. We desire to help them grow, become stronger, more social, more confident throughout not only their exercising experiences, but also in life itself. We have seen how even a little motivation combined with the right experience of the person next to you can make an enormously positive impact on one’s life. That is our aim, and our mission.

Meet new people

Every Saturday we host an “Outdoor Boot Camp”. This outdoor activity is a great opportunity to meet new people from Aarhus and find out if social exercising is your thing. This one hour session includes all kinds of exercises involving all your body parts into work-out. And you don’t need to worry if you are not a pro- we train people with different levels of advancement. Not being able to keep up with a certain tempo is just as big of a part of exercising as being a seasoned pro. We’re all about learning, and there is no better teacher than raw experience.
